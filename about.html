About Me



Hello! My name is Nikhil Tigga. I am currently pursuing a Master's in Computer Applications (MCA) from BIT Mesra, Ranchi. With a strong foundation in web development, Java, C++, C, SQL, and Data Structures, I have developed a deep passion for technology and programming. My academic journey has been filled with learning and growth, and I am constantly seeking new challenges to enhance my skills and knowledge.

Apart from my technical expertise, I have a diverse range of interests that I love to explore and share. One of my greatest passions is writing. Through my blog, I enjoy sharing productivity hacks that can help others optimize their time and achieve their goals. I also write destination guides, providing insights and tips for travelers looking to explore new places. Additionally, I review movies and TV shows, offering my perspective on the latest releases and hidden gems.

Travel and photography are two of my favorite hobbies. There is something incredibly fulfilling about discovering new destinations and capturing their beauty through the lens of my camera. Each photograph tells a story and preserves memories of the wonderful places I have visited. Whether it's a bustling cityscape, a serene landscape, or a cultural festival, I strive to convey the essence of the moment through my photos.

Hobbies and crafts also play an important role in my life. Engaging in creative activities such as painting, DIY projects, and crafting allows me to unwind and express myself in different ways. These activities provide a perfect balance to my tech-driven pursuits and keep my creative juices flowing.

I am always open to new opportunities and collaborations. If you are interested in working together or have any inquiries, please feel free to reach out to me through the contact section of my website. Thank you for taking the time to learn more about me. I hope you find my blog informative and enjoyable.
